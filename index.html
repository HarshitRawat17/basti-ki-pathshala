About Us

Basti ki Pathshala is a grassroots NGO dedicated to making quality education accessible for children living in underprivileged slum areas (bastis) in India.

Our journey began with a few passionate volunteers and a handful of curious children gathering beneath a tree. Today, we conduct open classrooms in multiple neighborhoods, creating safe and joyful learning spaces.

  • After-school teaching in Maths, Science, Languages, and Arts
  • Awareness sessions on hygiene, life skills, and social values
  • Community events: festivals, parent meetings, and more

We believe every child deserves a chance to learn and dream. Be a part of our mission—let’s build brighter futures together!
